The Hidden Dragon Inn and Tavern

About

Welcome all!

Founded during original player housing, The Hidden Dragon Inn is a cozy spot nestled in the heart of Xenos, the largest city on the isle of Elysium. Just a block east of the forum and right on the coast of The Pleasure Lagoon. With free rooms to rent and an active local community, it is the perfect spot to call home! Oh, and did we mention that every guest receives a discount on ale?! If interested in renting a room, please click here.

Beer, BEER, BEER!

Speaking of ale, The Hidden Dragon Inn is home to one of the finest culinary grandmasters in all of New Britannia. With all of the current in game culinary recipes to choose from, head on over to the vendor at the front door to purchase whatever you need to get you through those long New Britannian nights. For more info and prices, please check out the menu.

Location

The inn is located in the town of Xenos. Xenos can be found on the island of Elysium. To get to Elysium take a ferry from either Aerie or Etceter. Once in Xenos, head to the forum in the middle of the town. On there, simply go one block east towards the north. The inn backs up to Oden's house (all +5 crafting stations). The Hidden Dragon boasts stellar views of Pleasure Lagoon and the Bay of Storms.
