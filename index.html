About

Welcome all!

The Hidden Dragon Inn is a cozy spot nestled in the heart of Xenos, the largest city on the isle of Elysium. Just a block east of the lottery auditorium and right on the coast of Sheltered Cove. With free rooms to rent and an active local community, it is the perfect spot to call home! Oh, and did we mention that every guest receives a discount on ale?! If interested in renting a room, please click here.

Beer, BEER, BEER!

Speaking of ale, The Hidden Dragon Inn is home to one of the finest culinary grandmasters in all of New Britannia. With all of the current in game culinary recipes to choose from, head on over to the vendor at the front door to purchase whatever you need to get you through those long New Britannian nights. For more info and prices, please check out the menu.
